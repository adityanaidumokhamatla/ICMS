Journey of INDIA

1947

Independence from British rule

In 1947, India gained independence from British rule, marking the end of nearly two centuries of colonialism.

1950

Adoption of the Constitution of India

The period following independence saw the drafting and adoption of the Constitution of India in 1950, laying the foundation for a democratic republic.

1950s and 1960s

Struggles Between 1950s and 1960s

Throughout the 1950s and 1960s, India focused on nation-building efforts, including economic development, infrastructure expansion, and social reforms.

1970s and 1980s

Periods of political turbulence

The 1970s and 1980s witnessed periods of political turbulence, economic challenges, and social movements, shaping India's trajectory as a diverse and vibrant democracy.

1991

Rapid economic growth

In 1991, India initiated economic liberalization reforms, ushering in an era of globalization and rapid economic growth.

21st century

Major global player

By the turn of the 21st century, India emerged as a major global player, known for its technological advancements, cultural richness, and growing influence on the world stage.
